Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this action-packed adventure game. How long can you last?

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where your skills and strategy determine how long you can last against endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and fight to survive in this action-packed adventure.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities and upgrades as you level up
  • Survive as long as possible!

Game Features

  • Multiple character classes with unique abilities
  • Diverse enemy types with different behaviors
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Various power-ups and upgrades
  • Real-time combat system
